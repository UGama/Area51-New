## Company summary
Area 51 World operates indoor family entertainment centres in Queensland and New South Wales.
Public offerings include age-zoned play, soft play, slides, trampolines, climbing, ropes, toddler zones, cafés, birthday parties, school/group bookings, gift cards, selected adult-only sessions, selected VR experiences and selected snow attractions.

HQ contact
- Phone: 07 4351 5151
- Email: askus@area51world.com.au
- General enquiries: 9:00 am–9:00 pm daily
- Party enquiries: 9:00 am–5:00 pm Monday to Friday

Booking basics
- Online booking is strongly recommended; walk-ins may be accepted but not guaranteed.
- Grip socks are mandatory on play equipment. Area 51 socks are required and previously purchased socks may be reused.
- A waiver is required for every participant.
- Guests should arrive about 15 minutes before booked sessions.
- Children must not be left unattended.
- Adults supervising ages 5–12 must have their own ticket if they participate on equipment.
- Outside food is generally prohibited. Refillable water bottles and nut-free birthday cakes are usually permitted with venue approval.
- Only nut-free cakes are allowed; cakeage fees may apply.
- Venues are wheelchair accessible and a carer may enter free with a valid Companion Card.
- Smoking and vaping are prohibited.

Safety and restrictions
- Staff enforce attraction-specific rules; participants must follow instructions.
- Remove glasses, jewellery and loose objects where instructed.
- No rough play, racing, diving, flips, sharp objects, food or drink on equipment.
- People with injuries, heart/respiratory conditions, pregnancy, sprains or broken bones may be excluded from some attractions.
- Many attractions use age, height and weight limits.

## Ticketing and pricing notes
- Prices and session lengths vary by venue, day type and age.
- Prices are a snapshot and should be verified at checkout.
- Underwood, Helensvale, Mt Gravatt, Redcliffe, Ipswich, Castle Hill and Strathpine may have different ticket categories for toddlers, kids, older kids, Power Play and adults.
- Infant tickets (0–11 months) are often free with another paying guest; otherwise a low infant ticket may apply.
- Power Play, Adults Only, VIP/The Lot and multi-hour sessions are special products with additional restrictions and inclusions.

## Party bookings
- Birthday parties include a private party room, party host, play time, tableware, wristbands, socks and a food pack.
- Typical packages: Classic, Ultimate, Deluxe.
- Venue party minimums are usually 10 guests; capacity and package details vary by location.
- Party deposits are non-refundable; payments are generally non-refundable.
- Parties may require final guest numbers and payment several days before the event.
- No-show guests are typically forfeited.
- Parents should arrive about 30 minutes early for party setup and 15 minutes early for general bookings.
- Only nut-free cakes, candles, weighted balloons and party bags are generally allowed.

## Group bookings and school events
- Group bookings generally start at 20 guests.
- Group offers may require 50% deposit and full payment several days before the visit.
- Add-ons and changes often close days before the event.
- Group bookings are usually not refundable within seven days.
