How to use
  1. Place the iPhone or iPad where it can see the queue.
  2. Tap Start camera and allow camera access.
  3. Drag on the camera image to draw a green queue zone.
  4. People whose box centre is inside the zone are counted.

For best results, keep the device fixed, avoid backlighting, and frame people from at least waist height.

Video stays on this device. This app does not upload or store camera footage.
